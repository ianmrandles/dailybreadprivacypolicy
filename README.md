# Privacy Policy

**Daily Bread — AI Study Bible**

*Last updated: May 20, 2026*
*Effective date: May 20, 2026*

This Privacy Policy explains how Daily Bread (the "App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use Daily Bread — AI Study Bible on iPhone, iPad, or other Apple devices. We believe in being plain about what we do with your data. If anything here is unclear, please contact us at the address at the bottom of this page.

---

## 1. A quick summary

Here's the short version, in plain English:

- **You can use Daily Bread without an account.** Reading plans, highlights, notes, and your daily streak work without us knowing who you are.
- **If you create an account** (with Apple Sign-In, Google, or email), we collect basic profile information so your data can sync across your devices.
- **The AI Study feature** sends your questions to one or more trusted third-party AI providers (currently Anthropic and OpenAI). We don't use your conversations to train AI models, and these providers don't use API data submitted by our application to train theirs.
- **We don't sell your data** to advertisers or anyone else.
- **You can delete your account and your data** at any time from within the App.

## 2. Information we collect

### Information you provide directly

- **Account information (optional).** If you choose to create an account, we collect your name, email address, and authentication identifier from Apple, Google, or our own sign-up form.
- **Content you create.** Your highlights, notes, reading plan progress, prayer plan responses, AI chat history, and personal preferences are stored so the App can show them back to you.
- **Communications.** If you contact us for support, we keep your messages and our replies so we can help you.

### Information collected automatically

- **Device and usage information.** We may collect your device type, operating system version, App version, language, time zone, and general usage patterns (for example, which features are used most). We do not collect your precise location.
- **Crash and diagnostic data.** If the App crashes or experiences an error, we receive a report so we can fix the issue. These reports do not include the content of your scripture reading, highlights, or AI conversations.
- **Anonymous identifiers.** We use anonymous identifiers (not tied to your name or email) to count installs and measure feature engagement in aggregate.

> We do not use third-party advertising trackers. Daily Bread does not display ads.

## 3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide the core features of the App — scripture reading, prayer plans, highlights, notes, daily streaks, and AI Study
- Sync your content across your devices when you're signed in
- Personalize your reading plans based on the topics, values, and focuses you select
- Fix bugs, improve performance, and develop new features
- Respond to your support requests
- Process subscription payments (handled by Apple — see Section 6)
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Use

## 4. The AI Study feature

Daily Bread includes an AI Study feature that lets you ask questions about scripture and receive answers grounded in the biblical text and historic Christian tradition. Because this is the part of the App most people have questions about, we want to be especially clear:

### Who powers the AI

The AI Study feature is powered by large language models operated by trusted third-party AI providers. Currently, we use:

- **Anthropic, PBC** — maker of Claude. See [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy).
- **OpenAI, L.L.C.** — maker of GPT. See [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy).

When you submit a question to AI Study, your message is transmitted to one of these providers, processed by their model, and the response is returned to you through our App. You will not see which provider answered any particular question — from your perspective, it is simply "AI Study." We may add, remove, or change providers over time as we improve the feature; we will update this Policy to reflect material changes.

### What gets sent

When you use AI Study, we send the selected provider the text of your question, the verse or passage you're studying, and instructions that constrain the model's responses to scripture and traditionally received teachings of the Church. We do *not* send these providers your name, email, account identifier, location, or any other identifying information about you.

### How these providers use your data

Our AI providers process your messages to generate responses. Under our commercial agreements with them, data submitted through our application is not used to train their AI models. Each provider applies its own security, retention, and abuse-monitoring practices to the data we send. To understand each provider's practices in detail, please review the privacy policies linked above.

### How we use this data

We retain your AI Study conversation history on our servers so you can review it later within the App. We may review aggregated, anonymized usage data to improve the AI Study experience — for example, identifying common question types so we can make the feature work better. We do not read individual user conversations except when required to investigate abuse, a safety issue, or a specific support request you initiate.

### Important limits on AI Study

AI Study is a study tool, not a spiritual authority, counselor, or pastor. Its answers are generated by software and may contain errors. Daily Bread does not provide medical, mental health, legal, or pastoral advice through the AI feature. If you are in crisis, please contact a qualified professional or trusted local pastor.

## 5. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

- **Service providers.** We share data with third parties that operate our infrastructure, including our AI providers (currently Anthropic and OpenAI — see Section 4), and the providers of our hosting, authentication, and crash-reporting services. These providers are contractually limited to using your information only to provide services to us.
- **Apple.** Subscription payments are processed by Apple under its own privacy policy. We receive information about your subscription status but never your full payment details.
- **Legal requirements.** We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or other legal process, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect rights, prevent fraud, or address a safety issue.
- **Business transfers.** If Daily Bread is acquired by or merged with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
- **With your consent.** We may share information for other purposes if you have given us permission to do so.

## 6. Subscriptions and payments

Daily Bread offers a Premium subscription for $6.99 per month, which unlocks unlimited AI Study chat, advanced original-language tools, and other premium features.

All subscription payments are processed by **Apple** through your App Store account. We never see or store your credit card number, billing address, or other payment credentials. Apple handles payment, billing, refunds, and renewals according to its own terms and privacy policy.

Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your Apple ID account settings.

## 7. How long we keep information

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App to you. Specifically:

- **Account information:** retained until you delete your account.
- **Content (notes, highlights, AI conversations, plan progress):** retained until you delete it within the App or delete your account.
- **Diagnostic and crash reports:** retained for up to 90 days.
- **Support communications:** retained for up to 2 years.

If you use Daily Bread without an account, your data is stored only on your device. Uninstalling the App deletes that data.

## 8. Your rights and choices

### Everyone

- **Access your data.** You can view all of your content within the App at any time.
- **Delete your data.** You can delete individual notes, highlights, and conversations within the App. Account holders can delete their entire account from Settings → Account → Delete Account.
- **Use the App without an account.** All core features are available without signing in.
- **Control notifications and tracking.** Use your iOS Settings to control push notifications and App Tracking Transparency permissions.

### California residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you live in California, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, to correct inaccurate information, and to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

### European Economic Area, UK, and Switzerland (GDPR)

If you live in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the right to access, rectify, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data, the right to data portability, and the right to object to processing. The legal bases for our processing are: performance of a contract (providing the App), legitimate interests (improving the App, preventing fraud), and consent (where required). You may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

## 9. Children's privacy

Daily Bread is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can delete it.

Users under the age of 18 should use Daily Bread only with the involvement of a parent or guardian. The AI Study feature is not designed for use by children and should be used under adult supervision.

## 10. Security

We use industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews. No system is perfectly secure, however, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data. If we become aware of a security incident affecting your information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

## 11. International users

Daily Bread is operated from the United States. If you use the App from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you within the App or by email. Your continued use of Daily Bread after the changes take effect means you accept the revised policy.
